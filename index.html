His work are notable for their pasmetic style of writing. He are best known for one nevel, “Brave New World” (1932).

J. R. R Tolkien

John Ronald Reuel Tolkien, born on 3rd of January 1892 in Bloemfontein, South Africa. Died on the 2nd of September 1973 in Bournemouth, Dorset, Great Britain. He was a british author, philologist and an professor at a University. He wrote a lot of big titles as, The Lord of The Rings (1954) and also The Hobbit (1937).

Josep Conrad

Joseph Conrad was born 1857 and died 1924. He was born as Józef Teodor Konrad Korzeniowski, in Russian-occupied Polish Ukraine. He was known as a master mariner with his own ship. Conrad was regarded as one of the greatest writers of British fiction – with English as his third language. One of his most popular books is Heart of Darkness, it speaks of how dark and cold the war could be. His speciality was novels and short stories.
