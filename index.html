D.H Lawrence

David Herbert Lawrence was born at the 11th September 1885 in the United Kingdom, in Eastwood. He later on died at the 2nd of Mars 1930 in Franche. Lawrence believed that humans and nature had a deep connection, even though the industrialised society.
